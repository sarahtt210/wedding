How We Met

As much as we both wish we could say we met in person and our eyes locked on the beach, that is simply not the case. Instead, we both swiped right. This started a few weeks of talking before Anthony's joke about going to sushi one night ended up with Sarah saying "bet" and meeting for sushi that night. After that there were many late nights talking and enough dates to have people asking if we were ever going to get tired of spending that much time together. Spoiler alert, we never did.

Let the Adventures Begin

After we started dating the adventures truly began. We bonded over our love of food, the San Diego Padres, being outdoors, and binging random TV shows. Over the next 3.5 years we shared in many ups, downs, and changes. Including 1 graduation, 7 job changes, 6 moves, and 1 new family member our furbaby Beau. Throughout all of this chaos there has always been one constant, our love for each other.

How we got Engaged

After almost 3 years of dating, the time had finally come. Anthony knew it was always going to be memorial day weekend, although he would never let Sarah believe that. Instead he spent many months taunting her that she would have to wait till after his cousings wedding that coming July. However, he had always heard how special that weekend and Idyllwild is to Sarah, so he knew it was the perfect place to pop the question. After a weekend filled with memories and family, Anthony took Sarah for a walk on Strawberry creek and the rest is history.
